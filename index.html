In Loving Memory of Michael Jackson

August 29, 1958 - June 25, 2009

Biography

Michael Jackson, known as the "King of Pop," was born on August 29, 1958, in Gary, Indiana. He began his musical career at a young age as the lead singer of the Jackson 5, a group formed with his brothers. His solo career took off in the 1970s with groundbreaking albums like Off the Wall and Thriller, the latter becoming the best-selling album of all time. Known for his distinctive voice, innovative dance moves like the moonwalk, and contributions to pop culture, Jackson became a global icon. Michael Jackson passed away on June 25, 2009, but his artistry continues to inspire generations worldwide.
